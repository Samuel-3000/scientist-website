Professor Avi Loeb

Professor Avi Loeb is the scientist that I chose for this project. The reason that I chose Professor Loeb is because of his hypothesis that an Extraterrestrial spacecraft entered our solar system in 2017. I agree with Professor Loeb that this object could have been an Extraterrestrial spacecraft of some sort. Professor Loeb's hypothesis came about when the reflective and tumbling "'Oumuamua" object entered our solar system in 2017, and then rapidly exited our solar system, somehow accelerating against the Sun's gravitational pull. The true nature of the 'Oumuamua object is still a mystery and various hypotheses have been proposed regarding it's origin and composition. The detection of 'Oumuamua could be compared to the interplanetary equivalent of knocking on someone's door and then running away before they could see you.
